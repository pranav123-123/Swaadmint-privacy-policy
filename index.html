1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile picture (when using Google Sign-In)
  • User Content: Recipe titles, descriptions, ingredients, cooking steps, and images you upload
  • Preferences: Favorite recipes, dietary preferences, and cooking interests

1.2 Usage Information

  • App Analytics: Recipe views, search queries, feature usage, and app performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support
  • Location Data: General location for regional recipe recommendations (iOS only, with permission)

1.3 Permissions We Request

  • Camera & Photo Library: To upload recipe images
  • Notifications: For daily recipe reminders and updates
  • Microphone: For voice search functionality (iOS only)
  • Location: For regional recipe recommendations (iOS only)

2.2 AI Assistant Features

  • Process your recipe requests through Google Gemini AI

5.1 Data Protection

5.2 Access Controls
